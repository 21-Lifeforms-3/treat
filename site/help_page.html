Helpful Hints

COSTUME / HOME:

Click through costume choices until you see one you want to wear.

Click through house styles. This will be your home that others visit for trick-or-treating.

Decorations for your yard are stored in your cupboard and can be applied to your house.

No need to click for submission, they will automatically be entered as your choices when you join.

JOINING:

Click the Player Name button to generate a random name. Click again to change it.

Click House Title button to give your home a title. Click again to change it.

Your e-mail address is important to keep your game secure.

Enter a unique password.

Set fright intensity using the slide bar.

You are good to go... press JOIN to begin your adventure.

Trick-or-Treating:

Walk to town and visit houses to gather treats

Your home page keeps track of how much candy you have available for trick-or-treaters.

More candy can be added to front door from your cupboards.

Be aware that if no candy is found visitors may toss eggs, toilet paper, or some other trickery on your house.

You have your own available suppies for tricks are in your backpack.

TOP AWARDS:

The awards page lists top players in the following categories:

Gathered the biggest sack of treats.

Supplied more candy to trick-or-treaters.

The greatest trickter.

I NEED MORE:

More treats and tricks can be purchased at the store front.

All item bundles are 25 cents. The proceeds will help fund the upkeep of the site.

Pay-pal and Facebook-funds are accepted and secure.

YOUR PHOTO:

On your home page you will find an option to upload a photo of youself in costume.

It is always fun to dress up and show off your own costuming.

Please note that inappropriate photos will be removed along with your page.
